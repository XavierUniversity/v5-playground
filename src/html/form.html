Make Your Commitment

We're excited you're taking the next step toward joining the Xavier family - a group of 4,600 undergraduate students and 72,000 alumni. It's a community of values, achievement and care of the whole person. Of course, it wouldn't be the same without you.

Incoming students who wish to cancel their enrollment at Xavier, excluding those admitted to the College of Nursing, may request a refund of their deposit in writing or via email by May 1. All deposits submitted for students admitted to the College of Nursing are non-refundable.

Please complete the information below to pay your tuition deposit and/or housing deposit for Fall 2019, Spring 2020, or Fall 2020.

Deposit Information

If you are planning on commuting, select Tuition. If you will be living on campus (required for traditional-age freshmen and sophomores who live more than 35 miles from campus), select Tuition and Housing.

Student Information

Xavier accepts electronic checks and the following credit cards: American Express, Discover, MasterCard and Visa. All credit card transactions will be assessed a convenience fee. There are no additional fees for paying by electronic check. To pay by a physical check download the mail-in deposit form.

If you have any questions regarding your tuition/housing payment, feel free to contact the office of admission at 877-XUADMIT (982-3648) or 513-745-3301. Email us at xuadmit@xavier.edu.

Having trouble submitting your deposit? Please call us at 513-745-4296 between the hours of 8am and 4pm EST for further support.
