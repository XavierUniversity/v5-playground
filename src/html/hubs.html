In celebration of Earth Day, the Xavier Sustainability Committee and the Brueggeman Center will be hosting Michael Fiore, Director for Environmental, Occupational Health and Safety at NorthShore University HealthSystem in Evanston, IL for a presentation on "Health/Wellness, Justice, and Sustainability." Fiore will discuss how NorthShore has undertaken efforts to promote both patient care and environmental wellness cooperatively, with opportunity for questions afterward. The event is free and open to the public and will take place on Wednesday, April 15th at 7 PM in the Gallagher Clocktower Lounge.

What can you eat during Lent?

To help those participating, on-campus dining will have options to assist in your time of fasting, prayer, and reflection.

On Ash Wednesday, Good Friday and all Fridays during Lent, when you cannot eat meat, there are many great options. You can still enjoy your meals, even without meat. To view the menus for Hoff Dining Commons, please visit xavier.edu/dining or click here. Gallagher Lower Level retail will have an added fish option as well as meatless options such as plant-based nuggets, beyond burgers, and made-to-order salads.

Order ahead and skip the line using the Mobile Ordering App. Accepts Dining Dollars, X Cash, and Credit/Debit. The app allows you to pick up ASAP or order ahead for a specific pick up time. Need the app? Download in the Apple Store or Google Play by searching "Transact Mobile Ordering."
